MDVORO

A hierarchical multiscale tool for molecular dynamics

MDVORO : A Hierarchical Multiscale Modelling Tool

MDVORO respects any input with a (x,y,z,type) data structure, allowing the user to scale spatial and temporal domains through a hierarchical multiscale model. Polymer chains greatly exceed the length of the control volumes, resulting in limited deformation lengths within molecular dynamics. Converting to a meshed volume allows us to explore continuum mechanics, representing a semi-crystalline polymer as a crystal in amorphous matrix.

Applications

Semi-crystalline Polymers

Paired with POTHOS, the alignment and clustering tool, semi-crystalline polymers can be meshed and represented in FEM as a binary or ternary composite, similar in theory to the Takayanagi Model.

Composites

Polymer-composites often result in the development of the "interphase", where polymer chains may align to and crystallize about nanofillers. MDVORO can mesh these regions seperately, and can be combined with other ABAQUS codes for further develoment.

Polycrystalline Metals

Paired with Ackland-Jones Analysis, polycrystalline metals may be meshed for representation in ABAQUS. This, paired with the composite capabilities, makes MDVORO a convenient method for hierarchical models of all types.
